Privacy Policy

Spend Wise application stores users’ financial expense and debt records locally on their device.

The application does not share personal data with third parties.

Data is kept only within the device using Room Database, and it is automatically removed when the application is uninstalled.

Users can delete all their data at any time by removing the application from their device.

Spend Wise does not collect or transmit any personal data over the internet. The Spend Wise app is not intended for children and does not contain any content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation. We have a zero-tolerance policy against such content.
