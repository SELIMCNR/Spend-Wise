Privacy Policy

Wise Spend is a personal finance tracker. The app stores expense and debt records locally on the user’s device using Room Database.

No personal data is collected, transmitted, or shared with third parties. All data is automatically removed when the app is uninstalled.

Users can delete their data at any time by uninstalling the app. Wise Spend does not provide financial services such as loans, credits, or payment processing. The app is not intended for children. It contains no content related to child sexual abuse or exploitation, and we enforce a strict zero‑tolerance policy against such content.
